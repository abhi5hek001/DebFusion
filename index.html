Use Cases/Features:

Export/Import

Import, edit, and combine sound files.
Export your recordings in many
different file formats,including
multiple files at once.

Effects

Our Audio Plug-in Manager Streamlines Effect
And Generator Installation, paving the Way
for Effortless Menu Customization 
tailored to your audio needs.

Plugins

Access a Variety of Plug-ins:
LADSPA, LV2, Nyquist, VST, and Audio Unit Effects.
Customize Nyquist Effects with Ease or
Create Your Own for Enhanced Audio.

Quality

Enjoy top-quality audio at 16-bit, 24-bit, and 32-bit levels.
Our advanced techniques ensure flawless sample rate
and format conversions for the ultimate sound experience.

Editing

Easy editing with Cut, Copy, Paste and Delete
Also unlimited sequential Undo (and Redo)
in the session to go back any number of steps.

Analysis

Spectrogram view mode for visualizing and
selecting frequencies. Plot Spectrum window for
detailed frequency analysis.
